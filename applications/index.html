Industrial Sectors

Reliable electrification for the industries that can't stop moving.

From open-pit mines to automated logistics centers, VOLT-X systems are engineered to keep crane power flowing in the harshest industrial environments.

SECTOR 01

Mining

Open-pit and underground mining operations depend on continuous crane power for material handling, even in dusty, high-vibration environments. IP23-rated housings and anti-condensation sections protect the system in extreme conditions.

SECTOR 02

Steel & Metals

Steel mills and metal processing plants require electrification systems rated for high heat and continuous duty cycles. VOLT-X's high-capacity series support up to 240A for the heaviest crane loads.

SECTOR 03

Cement

Cement plants combine dust, outdoor exposure, and constant material movement. VOLT-X conductor rails are built for indoor-to-outdoor transitions without electrical interruption.

SECTOR 04

Logistics & Manufacturing

Automated warehouses and manufacturing floors need precise, low-friction current collection for repetitive crane and hoist movement at scale.

SECTOR 05

Crane Integrators & Manufacturers

VOLT-X partners with crane and hoist manufacturers who need a certified, dimensionally consistent electrification system to specify into their builds.

SECTOR 06

Electromechanical Contractors

Installers and maintenance contractors rely on VOLT-X's self-locking hardware and standard 1.2m support spacing for faster, simpler installation.
