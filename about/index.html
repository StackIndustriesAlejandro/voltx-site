About VOLT-X

A distribution-first company, built for the long term.

VOLT-X LLC is a United States company specializing in the distribution of electrification systems for industrial travelling cranes — never selling direct to end clients, always through our authorized distributor network.

Who we are

VOLT-X LLC is headquartered in Albuquerque, New Mexico, USA. We distribute Powerail Enclosed conductor rail systems and related crane electrification components, sourced through a certified supply partnership and sold under our own VX product coding system.

How we sell

100% of VOLT-X sales go through the VOLT-X Authorized Grid (VAG) — our three-tier distributor program. We never sell direct to end clients. Distributors set their own resale pricing freely.
