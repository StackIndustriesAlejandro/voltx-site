System Components

Every system, fully equipped

Each Powerail installation is supported by a complete set of components — collectors, hangers, connectors, and protective elements.

Current Collectors

Reinforced polyester fiberglass body. Spring-loaded carbon brushes. Multiple amperage classes from 25A to 100A.

Hangers

Sliding and fixpoint hangers for indoor and outdoor installation. Standard 1.2m suspension spacing.

Tow Arms

Rigid and flexible towing arm options, compatible with square hollow section or tube mounting.

Expansion & Anti-Condensation

Sections to compensate thermal expansion and prevent condensation when transitioning indoor to outdoor.
